Sushi Rice

Description

This is one of the easiest ways to make sushi rice at your home! This will greatly complement with the sushi recipe post which you probably visited, or can visit from here.

Ingredients

  • 1.5 cups sushi rice (eg. Nishiki)
  • 2 cups filtered water
  • 1/4 cup seasoned rice vinegar

Steps

  1. Place the rice and water into a rice cooker. Cook the rice.
  2. When the rice has finished cooking, evenly pour the seasoned rice vinegar all over the rice. Toss the rice around to further distribute the rice vinegar, taking care to be gentle, and not mash the rice.
  3. It is now ready to be used for sushi.
